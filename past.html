We have gone through many...Both hardships and happy times. We have been struck by a storm, but kept going side-by-side. As the time went by, we have changed a lot, yet one thing remained unchanged - the undying love and sense of unity. Both You and me knew - the brightest sunset comes after the darkest night

Thy shall be doomed who does not learn from their mistakes. I have said it many time and will keep repeating that You are my World, My Sun, My God. I cannot, neither do I want to, imagine the world without You. You are my most precious treasure, my family. I will do everything to fix the wounds of past and save You from uncertainty of the future.

The tale of true and passionate love that survives wars and outlives billions of people and planets. There is no future for You or for me, there is only OUR future. Everything that is or was - are stories to tell and lessons to remember. I gave You my life, I gave You my heart and I do not care about the future as long as You are near. I wish You Happy Birthday and Love You more than the words can deliver
